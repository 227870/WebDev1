Salary

Current Revenue

In the 2010-11 season, the Vikings average ticket cost was $75. With 63,720 seats in the Metrodome, this brings in a revenue of $4.779 million per game.

Taxes

The state of Minnesota charges a 10 percent admission tax on Metrodome ticket sales, so this means that the state's annual tax revenue from ticket sales is about $4.779 million. The Vikings are also charged a 9.5 percent rent fee on each ticket sold, so that revenue was $4.54 million in 2010. Other rent was $0.749 million, so the total rent amount is $5.289 million.

Future Revenue in a New Stadium

Assuming that the tax rates don't change in a new stadium, we can then put a fairly-accurate guess into what the revenues should look like for the new stadium. The new stadium plans would have 65,000 seats with up to 7,500 club seats, so the average ticket price would likely increase.
