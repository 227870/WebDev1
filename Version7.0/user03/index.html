Categories of Endangered Animals

Critically Endangered:

Endangered:

These are animals that are at a high risk of becoming extinct in the wild or in general. They must pass a certain criteria to be put into this class

Vulnerable:

Must meet at least one of the five Red list criterea and being in decline of habitat or population

Near Threatend:

Are animals that are not considered Critically Endangered, Endangered or Vulnerable but experts say they will be listed as one of thoes categories in the near future

Least concerned:

These species don't face any population or habitat declline
