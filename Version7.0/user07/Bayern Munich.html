Best records

  • Bayern Munich has won the Bundesliga title 31 times (record)
  • Most points in a season: 91 (2012-13)(German Record)
  • Most Bundesliga wins: 1,158(record)

Fun facts about Bayern Munich

  • Since the foundation of the Bundesliga in 1963, Munich have won more than half of the league titles (29 out of 57).
  • Bayern scored 101 goals in the 1971/72 season, the most in the Bundesliga's history. In the 120th anniversary season of 2019/20, the men from Munich were just one goal away from equalling this record
  • The most clean sheets; FC Bayern managed 22 shutouts in the 2014/15 campaign, a Bundesliga record. Manuel Neuer was in goal for 32 games that season. In the other two matches, Pepe Reina was between the posts - and he didn't concede a single goal.

Team history

Bayern Munich was founded in 1900 and has become Germany's most famous and successful football club. Almost all of Bayern's success has come since the 1960s. Bayern Munich was formed when members of the MTV 1879 Munich sports club broke away to form their own club.
